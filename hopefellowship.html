Hope Fellowship

Hope Fellowship is a small church with a big heart for the Lord located in Bladen County, North Carolina, USA. They are a gathering of like-minded believers who share a desire to serve the community and reach out to those who are seeking Christ.

Initial Designs

Initially, I used a very customized fork of Snitz Forums as the foundation for a dynamic and interactive site. Over the years there were a number of different designs with a variety of different features.

In January 2007 Hope Fellowship started recording some of the sermons and made them available online for anyone to listen.

Moving to Google Apps

Because they were getting more attempted spam than any other site I'd worked on, we shifted platforms to Google Sites - a part of their Google Apps account.

The new site is far simpler than any previous iteration, however it retains the features that were actually used and adds a more useful calendar of events.

The official website is at http://public.hopefellowship-nc.org/

If you're interested, they have a number of sermons available for download.
