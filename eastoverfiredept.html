Eastover Fire Department

Eastover Fire Department was established in May of 1956. In May of 2006 they launched the new website.

Building The Site

I started with a very customized fork of Snitz Forums and used that as the foundation for a dynamic and interactive site. Since then there have been a few adjustments and minor features added.

Since this site was developed after I had built the Jesus Joshua 24:15 site, it was far easier to build a site the department could keep up on their own.

Updates, Etc.

With work and family and life in general, things have been fairly quiet on the site - for them and me. I'm working on an updated "base package" for building sites and plan on updating Eastover Fire Department's site with a new CMS and a new design as soon as the base package is finished.


Eastover Fire Department - Saving lives and protecting property since 1956
